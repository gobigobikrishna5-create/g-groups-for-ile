Leo (2023)

One of Vijay’s most intense performances, Leo is a modern action thriller with a deep emotional core.

Plot

A café owner with a hidden past is pulled back into a violent underworld to protect his family.

Key Highlights

Leo is a Tamil action thriller film released in 2023 and directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj. The movie stars Vijay in the lead role, along with Sanjay Dutt, Arjun Sarja, and Trisha Krishnan. The music for the film was composed by Anirudh Ravichander. The film is known for its stylish action scenes, powerful performances, and engaging storyline. One of the key highlights of Leo is the role played by Vijay, who appears as Parthiban, a simple café owner with a mysterious past. As the story develops, unexpected events reveal secrets about his identity, which creates suspense and excitement throughout the film. The movie also features strong villain characters played by Sanjay Dutt and Arjun Sarja. Another important highlight is that Leo is part of the Lokesh Cinematic Universe (LCU). The film attracted a huge audience and became a major success at the box office. Its energetic songs, including “Naa Ready” and “Badass,” and its intense action sequences made the movie very popular among fans of Vijay
