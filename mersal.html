Mersal (2017)

Vijay plays dual roles in this gripping social thriller that tackles corruption in the healthcare system.

Plot

When his family is threatened by a corrupt doctor, a man uses both brains and brawn to bring the system crashing down.

Legacy

Mersal is a Tamil action thriller film released in 2017 and directed by Atlee. The movie stars Vijay in the lead role, along with Samantha Ruth Prabhu, Kajal Aggarwal, and Nithya Menen. The music for the film was composed by A. R. Rahman. The film combines action, drama, and social messages, which made it very popular among audiences. One of the key highlights of Mersal is the powerful performance of Vijay, who plays three different roles in the film. Each character has a unique personality and purpose in the story. The movie also focuses on important social issues, especially problems in the medical system and the importance of affordable healthcare for people. Another major highlight is the film’s grand production, impressive visuals, and engaging story. The songs composed by A. R. Rahman, such as “Aalaporaan Thamizhan” and “Mersal Arasan,” became very popular. Because of its strong story, music, and performances, Mersal became a big commercial success and is considered one of the memorable films in the career of Vijay.
