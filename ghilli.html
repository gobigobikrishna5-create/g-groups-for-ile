Ghilli (2004)

One of Vijay’s biggest blockbusters, a high-octane action drama that redefined Tamil mass cinema.

Plot

Vijay stars as a street-smart Kabaddi player who becomes entwined in a fierce battle to rescue a girl from a dangerous gangster.

Why It Matters

As the story progresses, Velu faces many challenges while trying to keep Dhanalakshmi safe. Muthupandi and his men continue searching for them, which leads to several intense and action-packed moments in the film. Despite the dangers, Velu remains courageous and determined to protect her. Ghilli became a huge commercial success and was one of the biggest Tamil blockbuster films of 2004. The movie was loved by audiences for its exciting action scenes, Kabaddi sports theme, comedy, and energetic songs like “Appadi Podu” and “Arjunar Villu.” Even today, Ghilli is remembered as one of the most popular films in Vijay’s career.
